# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free project. Be constructive, assume good intent, discuss ideas rather than people, and make technical criticism specific and actionable.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, deliberate disruption, publishing private information, or sustained hostile conduct.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates this standard. Reports should be sent privately to the repository owner through their GitHub profile. Maintainers will review reports fairly and protect reporter privacy where possible.

## Scope

This code applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and public spaces where someone is representing the project.

This compact policy is inspired by the Contributor Covenant and may be replaced by a versioned Contributor Covenant as the community grows.
